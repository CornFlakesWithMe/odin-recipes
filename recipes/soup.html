Chicken Soup

Description

Chicken soup is a warm and comforting dish that is often made with tender pieces of chicken, flavorful broth, and vegetables such as carrots, celery, and onions. It is known for its rich aroma and soothing taste, making it a favorite meal during cold weather or when someone is feeling sick. The broth is usually light but full of flavor, and the chicken adds protein and heartiness to the dish. Some versions also include noodles, rice, or herbs to make it even more filling and delicious.

One of the reasons chicken soup is so popular is because it feels homemade and satisfying. It is a simple dish, but it can be prepared in many different ways depending on personal taste or family tradition. Some people prefer it thick and packed ith ingredients, while others enjoy a lighter, more broth-based version. No matter how it is made, chicken soup is often associated with comfort, care, and warmth, which is why it has remained a timeless favorite in many cultures around the world.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Broth
  • Pepper
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Onion
  • Salt
  • Noodles
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Add broth to a pot and heat it.
  2. Put in the chicken and let it cook.
  3. Add chopped carrots, celery, and onion.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Add noodles or rice if wanted./li>
  6. Let everything simmer until tender.
  7. Serve hot.
