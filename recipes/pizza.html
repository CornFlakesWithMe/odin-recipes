Pizza

Description

Pizza is a popular dish made with a round, flat crust that is baked until warm and slightly crisp. It is usually topped with tomato sauce, melted cheese, and a variety of ingredients such as pepperoni, sausage, mushrooms, onions, or peppers. The combination of the soft crust, rich sauce, and gooey cheese makes pizza both comforting and flavorful. Its smell alone is often enough to make people hungry, especially when it is fresh out of the oven.

One of the reasons pizza is so loved is its variety and flexibility. It can be simple with just cheese, or loaded with many toppings to create different flavors and textures. Some pizzas have a thin, crunchy crust, while others are thick and soft. People enjoy pizza at parties, family dinners, or even as a quick snack, and it is easy to share with others. Because it can be customized in so many ways, pizza remains one of the most popular foods around the world.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Roll out pizza dough
  2. Add tomato sauce on top of pizza dough
  3. Add cheese
  4. Add pepperoni
  5. Preheat oven to 350F
  6. Bake in oven for 25-30 minute
  7. Let to cool off for 2 minutes
